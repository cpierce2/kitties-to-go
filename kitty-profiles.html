Lovable Kitty Profiles

Titan's Story

Titan comes from a long line of lazy cats. He enjoys 18 hour naps on comfy Knitted blankets. When he is not sleeping, he is basking in the sun while dozing off in his favorite corner nook.

Titan is a perfect fit for anyone who seeks a long nap companion on a lazy Sunday.

Garfield's Story

Garfield is a sarcastic silly cat. He enjoys fresh homemade lasagna straight from the over. When he is not eating, he is sarcasticly picking on his housemates.

Garfield is a perfect fit for anyone who seeks a fine pasta dinner companion while enjoying some laughs.

Whiskers' Story

Whiskers is very playful and energetic. She loves playing with kids and chasing lasers. When she is not being playful, she is hunting at night for mice.

Whiskers is a perfect fit for anyone who lives on a farm or has young children.

Tom's Story

Tom is a mischevious cat. He enjoys mischief more than most and often you will find him going bouts his frienemy. When he is not cooking up plans to get back at Jerry, he is being chased by the dog in the yard.

Titan is a perfect fit for anyone who seeks a long nap companion on a lazy Sunday.

Simba's Story

Simba is the king of cats. He is destined to rule the savannah as well as your house. When he is not ruling the kingdom, he is often found enjoying some grubs with his two best friends Timon & Pumba.

Simba is a perfect fit for anyone who seeks adventure and off the menu snack items.
